# Requirements & Progress

## Requirements Overview
Build and maintain the CoreNexus IT services platform - a comprehensive business website with service pages, client portal, admin dashboard, blog, and SEO optimization for the Rome IT market.

## User Stories
- As a visitor, I can browse all IT services offered by CoreNexus
- As a visitor, I can request a free IT audit
- As a visitor, I can read blog articles about IT and cybersecurity
- As a visitor, I can see Google reviews for trust building
- As a client, I can log in and manage support tickets
- As an admin, I can manage users, tickets, and view analytics
